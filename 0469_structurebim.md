### Bienvenue à StructureBIM

A new French language Structural Engineering BIM blog is born:
[StructureBIM](http://structurebim.typepad.com).

It is mainly focused on Autodesk Revit Structure, Autodesk Robot Structural Analysis, AutoCAD Structural Detailing, and authored by Alexandre Mihalache, Olivier Bayle, Stphane Balmain and Emmanuel Lagardette.

Welcome to the Revit blogosphere!
